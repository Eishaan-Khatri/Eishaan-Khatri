### 👋 About

I work in the space between a research paper and a production system — training models that hold up under distribution shift, and shipping the pipelines that carry them. My interests sit at **robustness, evaluation, and honest metrics**: multilingual / code-mixed NLP, test-time adaptation, uncertainty, and AI-safety-adjacent empirical work.

- 🔬 **Published** in *Expert Systems with Applications* (Elsevier, Q1), CASML 2025, and ICDM.
- 🧪 **Currently** exploring model robustness under shift, LLM evaluation, and agentic systems.
- 🎓 Graduating 2026 · open to **research-associate / applied-scientist** roles.
- ✍️ I write about the messy middle of building ML — [blog](https://eishaan-khatri.github.io/blog.html).
